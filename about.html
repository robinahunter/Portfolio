about

My background in graphic design and product development has given me a unique perspective as a software engineer. I don't just focus on lines of code; I craft digital experiences that are not only functional but also beautiful and intuitive. I believe that technology should enhance and simplify our lives, just like a well-designed piece of outdoor gear should make our adventures more enjoyable.

When I'm not immersed in the world of software and design, you can usually find me exploring the great outdoors. Whether it's hiking through rugged mountain trails, paddling ocean currents, or simply camping under a starry sky, I find solace and inspiration in nature. There's something magical about the way the natural world operates, and I often draw parallels between the elegance of the code I write and the intricacies of the natural world.
